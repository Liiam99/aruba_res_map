Criteria

You define the weights of the criteria by dividing a maximum of 100 points between the four criteria. You are in charge to choose what is more important for the placement of renewable energy systems.

Rankings

Based on your weights for the criteria the locations will have different rankings, marked by three colours: green if it is a fitting location, yellow if it is an okay location, and red if it is an unsuitable location.

Descriptions

By clicking on a marker you can read the in-depth descriptions of all the criteria for that specific location.
